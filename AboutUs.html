About Us

Brewed Awakening, founded in 2025 by Stacy Ramdhan, at its convenient location in San Fernando is your one stop shop for all things TEA. Whether if its to stop in and purchase tea bags or coffee pods from our wide variety of specially selected teas and coffees or if its to stop in after a long hard day to sit in solace and enjoy a cup of warm tea by yourself to relax or even if its to meet a friend and catch up on the old days, then we are the tea shop for you! Our signature Desserts are also always freshly baked. The founder of this fine establishment always wanted a place where she can walk in and smell the smell of freshly brewed coffee, filled with the laughter of satisfied customers, families coming together for a meal or teenagers just coming by to study and enjoy a nice drink and because of her vision, today we are given "Brewed Awakening. We currently operate with limited staff at one location in San Fernando but we are presently in the process of opening a new branch in Port-Of-spain so that no one misses out on this amazing experience and flavours and soon, due to popular demand, there will be an implementation of curb side pick up for those persons who are unable to leave their vehicles or seem to be in a rush. All drinks are made before your for maximum satisfaction. All meals are freshly prepared by our in house chef that has been in the cooking business for the past 5 years, all mae with fresh products. All desserts are freshly baked as any left over desserts from said day is given to the less fortunate as our founder solely believes in giving.

Mission statement

To make the most delicious drinks, meals and desserts,to give customers the best experience, to guarantee customer satisfaction and a place of relaxation and comfort. To create a family friendly environment
