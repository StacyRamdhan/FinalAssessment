Brewed Awakening

Brewed Awakening is a tea shop perfect for Dining in or Take out. Fresh Tea is made daily along with succulent desserts that are perfect for a relaxing evening afterwork or a weekend. we also supply tea packets and mixtures in case you prefer to make at home.
